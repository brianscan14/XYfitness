Why XY?

Here at XYfitness, we prefer to instill the mentality of giving our clients the tools to better themselves. In a way we are giving them the fishing rod, as opposed to just the fish. What we mean by this is that following our methods and plans a client will eventually eliminate their bad habits subconsciously, and being healthier and fitter will just come naturally to them.

We do this through improving people's fitnes regime, with detailed exercise plans for all levels. Improving their eating habits, with easy, quick and delicious sample recipes included in our plans. Finally, improving their mindsets, along with these habitual changes it is very important the client believes they can achieve their goals.

The reason why Xfitness is so successful and our clients are so happy is because we undersatand that changing too many things at a time leads to a higher quit rate. Here it is our goal to change your regime, one small habit at a time, until it becomes part of your everyday thinking. This ultimately leads to our clients achieving their goals and indeed exceeding them, with the help of our plans.

How we work

1. Beginning

We completely understand that you may or may not be a "fitness expert" as everyone says. That is perfectly fine and our plans incorporate this. We do not expect you to be able to change your habits overnight, as this leads to a quicker 'quit' in the end.

2. Middle

Our plans try to instill into you one good habit at a time. This leads to less chance of giving up, as if these habits get slowly integrated into your lifestyle then you, the client are less likely to return your previous ones!

3. End

You will then be sustaining these good habits subconsciously, once this occurs you will be flying and your goals can be achieved. Slow and stead wins the race here and the easier you integrate these methods into your lifestyle the less likely your weight is to fluctuate.
